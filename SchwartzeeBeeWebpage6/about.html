We are beekeepers

We are beekeepers too. Schwartzee bees was founded in 2018 with a mission to help bees and make equipment that is better for the bee and beekeepers. Schwartzee bees is a small buissness based in Missouri, that started out with only two handmade hives, but has since grown to selling honey and other products.
